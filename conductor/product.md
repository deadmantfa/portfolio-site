# Product Definition - Wenceslaus Dsilva Portfolio

## Vision & Purpose
To create a fun, amazing, and professional personal portfolio website that positions Wenceslaus Dsilva as a premier candidate for high-level technology leadership roles (CTO/Chief Architect). The site will blend 20+ years of strategic leadership experience with cutting-edge frontend capabilities.

## Target Audience
- **Tech Recruiters and Hiring Managers:** Specifically those at top-tier product companies looking for seasoned leadership with deep technical roots.

## Primary Goals
- **Showcase Strategic Leadership:** Highlight 20+ years of technical leadership, strategic architecture, and team mentorship.
- **Demonstrate Technical Mastery:** Prove hands-on expertise with modern technologies like Three.js and Tailwind CSS through an immersive UI.
- **Optimize for Search:** Implement best-in-class SEO to rank for high-value keywords related to CTO and Software Architecture roles.

## Core Features
- **Interactive 3D Career Timeline:** A Three.js powered experience visualizing a 20-year journey from programmer to CTO.
- **Dynamic Skill Cloud:** A reactive visualization of a diverse tech stack including Python, PHP, AWS, Angular, and Serverless frameworks.
- **Project Deep Dives:** Detailed case studies for key career highlights such as the OnFees SAAS product and CashBoss mobile ecosystem.

## Unique Value Proposition (The Hook)
**The Visionary Architect:** Wenceslaus is presented not just as a manager, but as a leader who understands the "how" of deep system design and the "why" of strategic business alignment.
